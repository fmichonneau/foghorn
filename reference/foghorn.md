# CRAN check results at the terminal

`foghorn` brings the results of the CRAN checks into your R terminal.
This package is intended for developers that have published packages on
CRAN. The results of the checks can be summarized as a tibble (see
[`cran_results`](https://fmichonneau.github.io/foghorn/reference/cran_results.md)),
or as “pretty” terminal output (see
[`summary_cran_results`](https://fmichonneau.github.io/foghorn/reference/summary_cran_results.md)).
The package provides functions to view the logs of the CRAN check
results (see
[`cran_details`](https://fmichonneau.github.io/foghorn/reference/cran_details.md)),
or to visit the web pages hosted by CRAN (see
[`visit_cran_check`](https://fmichonneau.github.io/foghorn/reference/visit_cran_check.md)).
You can also use `foghorn` to follow your package during its submission
process to CRAN or to WinBuilder with,
[`cran_incoming`](https://fmichonneau.github.io/foghorn/reference/cran_incoming.md)
and
[`winbuilder_queue`](https://fmichonneau.github.io/foghorn/reference/winbuilder_queue.md).
